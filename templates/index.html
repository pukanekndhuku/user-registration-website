How to find?

When you run the `FREE VERSION.exe` script for the first time, your unique MAC Address is automatically copied to your clipboard.

Simply click in the box above and press Ctrl+V to paste it.

How to get?
You can get a free API key from Google.
  • Step 1: Go to Google AI Studio.
  • Step 2: Sign in with your Google account.
  • Step 3: Click the "Get API key" button.
  • Step 4: Click "Create API key in new project".
  • Step 5: Your key will be generated. Copy it and paste it into the box above.
